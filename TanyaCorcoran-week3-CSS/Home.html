Welcome to Fredericton!

Welcome to Fredericton! If this is your first time visiting the city, you’re in for a memorable experience filled with discovery and charm. Fredericton offers a unique blend of cultural attractions, historic landmarks, natural beauty, and vibrant community life. Whether you’re exploring its scenic trails, attending a lively festival, or wandering through its historic downtown, you’ll find plenty to see, do, and enjoy.

As the capital city of New Brunswick, Fredericton plays an important role in the province’s political, cultural, and educational landscape. Despite its status as a capital, it maintains a warm, small‑town feel—something that sets it apart from many other cities. Fredericton is known for its tree‑lined streets, riverside views, and friendly atmosphere, making it a welcoming place for both visitors and residents.

The city’s history is rich and diverse, shaped by Indigenous heritage, early European settlement, and centuries of development along the beautiful Saint John River. Historic sites, museums, and restored buildings help bring that story to life, offering visitors a chance to connect with the past while enjoying the comforts and creativity of the modern city.

From annual cultural celebrations and outdoor concerts to bustling markets and art galleries, Fredericton’s vibrant arts and culture scene reflects the spirit of the community. Whether you're interested in history, nature, food, music, or simply enjoying a peaceful walk by the river, Fredericton invites you to explore, learn, and experience everything it has to offer.
